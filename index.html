About me

I was borned in HK, which then travelled to and lived in a lot of places around the world due to parents working issues. I'm going to introduce the places I have been to and lived for.

HongKong

HongKong is the place where I was born and lived for about 9 years. HongKong is made up of an island and another part of land that is connected to mainland China called Kowloon. I lived on the island in a place called Central. It is one of the busiest places in HK, It is a beautiful city, places like Victoria Harbour and Causeway Bay are definitely worth a visit.

Naples

Naples is an Italian city that lies on the Southern Coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is the third largest city in Italy, just after Rome and Milan. I moved there with my mom in 2014 because of her working necessity for a year. It is a beautiful city with cozy weather and full of people with hospitality. I loved the football club there called Napoli, who was playing well in Serie A(Italian Football league). It was quite the reason why I chose to play soccer later on.

Nagoya

Nagoya is a big city in Japan that locates on the Pacific Coast on central Hunshu(Japanese Island).It is one of Japan's largest port along with those of Tokyo, Osaka, Yokohama, Kobe, and Chiba. I lived there for about a year with my dad in order to get the Japanese permanent resident right. It has a long history with a lot of ancient temples and castles, definitely worth a visit.The food there is great as well, especially the sushis and tempuras.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a large metropolitan city located on the Southern estuary of the Yangtze River, with Huangpu river flowing through it into the East Sea. Although it is just slightly bigger than New York City, but has a population 3 times larger than NYC, approximately of 25 million(2018). I moved there with my family in fall, 2016, and lived there till summer,2019. The city has developed at a high speed since the 1980s, along with Beijing; Shenzhen; and Guangzhou, they are the biggest metropolitan city in China. The Huangpu river separates the city into two regions, the Eastern part Pudong and the Western part Puxi.

Toronto

Toronto is the capital of the province of Ontario. It is the most populous city in Canada, and the fourth populous city in North America. It is also recognized as one of the most multicultural and cosmopolitan cities in the world. I came to Canada in 2019 and studied in Lakefield College School. Whenever in holidays, I would go back to Toronto and live in my uncle's house. Last year during the Covid pandemic, the school closed and I wasn't able to go back home to Shanghai. My uncle was happy to let me stay over the summer and it was a pleasant experience.
