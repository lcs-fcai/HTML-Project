Nagoya

Nagoya is a big city in Japan that locates on the Pacific Coast on central Hunshu(Japanese Island).It is one of Japan's largest port along with those of Tokyo, Osaka, Yokohama, Kobe, and Chiba. I lived there for about 2 years with my dad in order to get the Japanese permanent resident right. It has a long history with a lot of ancient temples and castles, definitely worth a visit.
