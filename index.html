HongKong

HongKong is the place where I was born and lived for about 9 years. HongKong is made up of an island and another part of land that is connected to mainland China called Kowloon. It is a beautiful city, places like Victoria Harbour and Causeway Bay are definitely worth a visit.

Naples

Naples is an Italian city that lies on the Southern Coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is the third largest city in Italy, just after Rome and Milan. I moved there with my mom in 2014 because of her working necessity for a year. It is a beautiful city with cozy weather and full of people with hospitality.

Nagoya

Nagoya is a big city in Japan that locates on the Pacific Coast on central Hunshu(Japanese Island).It is one of Japan's largest port along with those of Tokyo, Osaka, Yokohama, Kobe, and Chiba. I lived there for about a year with my dad in order to get the Japanese permanent resident right. It has a long history with a lot of ancient temples and castles, definitely worth a visit.The food there is great as well, especially the sushis and tempuras.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a large metropolitan city located on the Southern estuary of the Yangtze River, with Huangpu river flowing through it into the East Sea. Although it is just slightly bigger than New York City, but has a population 3 times larger than NYC, approximately of 25 million(2018). I moved there with my family in fall, 2016, and lived there till present. The city has developed at a high speed since the 1980s, along with Beijing; Shenzhen; and Guangzhou, they are the biggest metropolitan city in China.
